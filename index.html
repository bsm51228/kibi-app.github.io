How It Works

01
Set Your Vehicle

Pick your make, model, and year from 49k+ EPA-verified vehicles. Kibi fills in your MPG, fuel type, and range automatically.

02
Enter Your Destination

Tell Kibi where you're headed. It calculates exactly where you need to stop based on your range, fuel level, and preferences.

03
Get the Best Exit

Kibi scores every exit on your route and picks the one stop that has gas, food, and a bathroom, all at one exit, every time.

04
Navigate & Track

Kibi launches Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze with all stops pre-loaded. It tracks your CO₂ and trip stats the whole way there.
