How It Works

Four steps to a smarter drive

Kibi wraps around your existing navigation — no turn-by-turn, no replacing what works. Just better stops, every time.

01

Set Your Vehicle

Pick from 49,000+ EPA-verified vehicles. Kibi fills in your MPG, fuel type, and range automatically.

02

Enter Your Destination

Type where you're headed. Kibi calculates where you need to stop based on your range and preferences.

03

Get the Best Exit

Kibi scores every exit on your route and surfaces the single best stop — gas, food, and a bathroom at one place.

04

Navigate & Track

Kibi launches Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze with stops pre-loaded. Tracks your CO₂ the whole way.
