Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Kibi is a free iOS app built and operated by Brett Moran.

Information We Collect

We collect your name, email address, vehicle information, trip history, and location data. Location is collected while a trip is active and used solely to track distance and calculate CO₂ emissions.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to provide Kibi's features — pitstop planning, CO₂ tracking, and trip history. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below.

Third Party Services

Kibi uses Google Places API to find pitstop locations, and Supabase to store your account and trip data. These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

Location Data

Location is only collected while a trip is active. We do not track your location in the background outside of active trips. Location data is never sold or shared.

Data Retention

Your data is stored until you delete your account. You may request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@drivekibi.com.

Children's Privacy

Kibi is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
